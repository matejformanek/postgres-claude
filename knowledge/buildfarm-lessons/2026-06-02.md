## Lesson for the watcher

In a post-beta-stamp quiet window the 24h buildfarm slice may legitimately
contain **fewer than the 3-5 target failures**. Reporting one well-characterized
failure plus an explicit flake-exclusion list is the honest outcome — do not
pad the count by reclassifying git-mirror flakes or stale (>24h) clusters as
new.
