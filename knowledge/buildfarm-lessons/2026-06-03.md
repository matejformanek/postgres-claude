## Lesson for the watcher

The `unicorn pg_amcheckCheck` failure has now persisted **three consecutive
days** across unrelated commits (incl. the beta1 stamp). A failure that survives
that many unrelated trees is almost certainly **animal-local**, and re-reporting
it each day as "substantive" adds noise. Recommendation: once a failure is
confirmed to span ≥3 unrelated commits on a single member, **demote it to the
standing-noise list** with a back-reference, and spend the budget on genuinely
new signals (here: the simultaneous REL_17/REL_18 `unicorn` Build breakage, which
*is* a new event worth one line).
