## Confirmed root cause

### shelduck · master · Build (2026-07-18 13:12 **and** 2026-07-19 12:45)

**Real portability regression, recurring two days.** `[verified-from-log]`
Build of `src/common/file_utils.c` fails at `src/include/c.h:1374`:

```
error: expected expression before 'const'
  (StaticAssertVariableIsOfTypeMacro(expr, const underlying_type), \
```

Toolchain: **GCC 4.8.5 on SLES 12 SP5, s390x**. A recent commit (`d15a6bc`,
outside today's watch window) replaced `__builtin_types_compatible_p` with C11
`_Generic` in the type-checking macros in `c.h`. GCC 4.8.5 predates usable C11
`_Generic` support, so `unconstify()` (used by `file_utils.c`) no longer
compiles. Either the minimum-compiler bar moved or the macro needs a
pre-C11 fallback. Recurs on both days → not a flake; a genuine old-toolchain
break to watch for a follow-up fix or an animal-config bump.
