## Takeaways for the corpus

- The two in-window failures are **both persistent animal-specific
  conditions** (dikkop ECPG on FreeBSD/clang; unicorn pg_amcheck on MSVC) —
  neither is a master regression, and unicorn's ≥10-day pg_amcheck red streak
  is worth an owner ping.
- **No buildfarm signal ties to the 5 tip commits.** The type-hygiene series
  is behavior-neutral; the ECPG/pg_amcheck failures predate it and land on
  unrelated code.
- Pattern worth remembering: a **Build**-stage (not test-stage) failure on a
  single animal for a plausibly-risky commit (Andres's bufmgr fix,
  guaibasaurus) is the one entry here that *could* have been real — the
  discriminator is cross-animal cascade. No cascade → transient env. That
  heuristic (Build failure + no cascade + common toolchain = env, not source)
  is the reusable lesson.
