- ideal answer outline: confirm the `BufferIsLocal()` branch contract in
  `knowledge/subsystems/storage-buffer.md` and `knowledge/files/src/backend/storage/buffer/bufmgr.c.md`;
  point at `MarkBufferDirtyHint` / `LocalBufferAlloc` paths and the
  `GetBufferDescriptor` vs `GetLocalBufferDescriptor` invariant.
