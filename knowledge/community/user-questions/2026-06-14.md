## 2. Make printtup a bit faster

- **message-id:** https://www.postgresql.org/message-id/87ik81p7c0.fsf%40163.com
- **list:** pgsql-hackers
- **summary:** Optimizes `printtup` (the per-row client-output path) by reusing
  a permanent `FunctionCallInfo` plus an optional context instead of
  re-initializing fmgr state for every tuple, with type-specific fast paths for
  int2/int4/int8/float/numeric/text/date/time/timestamp. Reports 20-29% latency
  wins for binary format and 8-18% for text, biggest on small integers.
  `[verified-by-code via thread]`
- **Skill tag:** `fmgr-and-spi` (FunctionCallInfo reuse / output-function calls)
  + `executor-and-planner` (DestReceiver / per-tuple output path).
- **Ideal answer outline:** cite `source/src/backend/access/common/printtup.c`;
  explain the `FunctionCallInfoBaseData` reuse idiom from `knowledge/idioms/fmgr.md`
  and why per-tuple `InitFunctionCallInfoData` is hot; tie the DestReceiver
  contract to `knowledge/architecture/executor.md`.
