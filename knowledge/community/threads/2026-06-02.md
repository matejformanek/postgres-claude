## 2. POC: enable logical decoding when wal_level = 'replica' without a server restart — Masahiko Sawada

URL: `/message-id/CAD21AoAY1RBA0rVNQ74TtBPeq6uYFwcdrW4qYbytCEUwx_2Szw%40mail.gmail.com`

- Proof-of-concept letting a cluster start logical decoding without the usual
  `wal_level = logical` + restart, by promoting WAL detail dynamically. `[from-mailing-list]`
- Would remove a long-standing operational papercut: enabling logical
  replication today requires a restart and full-cluster WAL overhead. `[inferred]`
- Companion thread same day: "Fix race during concurrent logical decoding
  activation" (Sawada) — activation races are the hard part. `[from-mailing-list]`

**Why it matters:** WAL-level semantics and slot activation are foundational
to logical replication. See [[knowledge/architecture/replication.md]] and the
`replication-overview` skill.
