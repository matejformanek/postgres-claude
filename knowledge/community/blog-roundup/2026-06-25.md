## 1. All Your GUCs in a Row: enable_distinct_reordering and enable_group_by_reordering

- Author: Christophe Pettus. 2026-06-25 01:00. <https://postgr.es/p/9nj>
- TL;DR: Per-GUC explainer on the planner's ability to reorder DISTINCT and
  GROUP BY keys to cut comparison cost and skip sorts — optimizations added in
  PG17/18 that are usually invisible.
- Notes these reorderings "usually stay invisible" but can be toggled off to
  diagnose plan changes; pairs with the rest of the enable_* GUC series.
- Read if you care about: planner GUCs, GROUP BY / DISTINCT key ordering, sort

## 3. PostgreSQL Disaster Recovery with pgBackRest TLS Transport

- Author: Shridhar Khanal (Stormatics). 2026-06-25 09:45. <https://postgr.es/p/9nl>
- TL;DR: Walkthrough of configuring pgBackRest to ship backups/WAL over its TLS

  hardened DR pipeline.
- Frames it around a near-miss DR story, then covers the TLS server/cert setup
  and why TLS transport simplifies multi-host backup topologies vs SSH.
- Read if you care about: backup/PITR, pgBackRest operations, WAL archiving
