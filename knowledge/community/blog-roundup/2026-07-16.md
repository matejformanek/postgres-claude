## 4. LISTEN Carefully: How NOTIFY Can Trip Up Your Database — Jimmy Angelakos
- **URL:** https://postgr.es/p/9pH — 2026-07-15
- Production-incident analysis of `LISTEN`/`NOTIFY` contention: notification
  serialization drives lock cascades and a throughput cliff under high notify
  volume.
- Traces the async-notify queue and the commit-time serialization point that
  turns NOTIFY-heavy workloads into a bottleneck.
- **Read if you care about:** async notification internals, commit-time
  serialization, `AccessExclusive` lock cascades under NOTIFY.
  `[[knowledge/skills/locking]]`
