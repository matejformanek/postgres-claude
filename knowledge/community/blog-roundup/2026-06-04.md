## 3. All Your GUCs in a Row: cursor_tuple_fraction
- Christophe Pettus · 2026-06-04 01:00 UTC · <https://postgr.es/p/9l9>
- `cursor_tuple_fraction` defaults to 0.1 — the planner assumes only 10% of a
  cursor's rows get fetched, biasing toward fast-start plans.
- Can degrade when the application actually reads the whole result set.
- Read if you care about: planner cost knobs / fast-start vs. total-cost plans.
  cf. [[knowledge/subsystems/optimizer.md]].
