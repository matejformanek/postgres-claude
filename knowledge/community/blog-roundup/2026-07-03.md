## 1. Looking Forward to Postgres 19: Checksums For All

- **Author:** Shaun Thomas · **URL:** https://postgr.es/p/9o5 · 2026-07-03
- TL;DR:
  - Covers the PG 19 work to make data checksums toggleable **online** — an
    administrator can turn checksums on (or off) on a running cluster without the
    old offline `pg_checksums` downtime.

## 2. All Your GUCs in a Row: enable_nestloop

- **Author:** Christophe Pettus · **URL:** https://postgr.es/p/9n_ · 2026-07-03
- TL;DR:
  - Latest in the per-GUC series: what `enable_nestloop` actually does (it doesn't
    disable nested loops, it makes them cost-prohibitive), when the planner still
    picks a nested loop, and why it's "PostgreSQL's most notorious performance
    disaster" when a row-estimate goes wrong.
  - Practical framing of the cost-model lever and the misestimate failure mode
    (tiny estimate → nestloop → catastrophic on a large actual row count).
- **Read if you care about:** the planner cost model / join-method selection from
  the operator side — a useful lay companion to the join-hardness -hackers WIP in
  today's threads. `[[knowledge/idioms/cost-join-paths.md]]`,
