### 1. pg_kpart PostgreSQL extension
- **Author:** Gilles Darold · 2026-06-13 · <https://postgr.es/p/9lO>
- An extension that **enforces partition-key usage** in queries against
  partitioned tables — rejecting or flagging statements that would trigger a
  full scan across all partitions instead of pruning to one. `[inferred]`
- The mechanism is almost certainly a planner/executor hook that inspects the
  plan for the absence of partition pruning quals — a practical guardrail for
  large partitioned deployments. `[inferred]`
- **Read if you care about:** partition pruning, planner hooks, operational
  guardrails on partitioned schemas. cf.
  [[knowledge/subsystems/partitioning.md]] (`extension-development` skill for
