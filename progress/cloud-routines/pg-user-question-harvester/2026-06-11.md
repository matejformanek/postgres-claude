## Note for the recipe maintainer

The recipe's Inputs section gives the month-archive URL as
`.../pgsql-general/<YYYY>/<MM>/`. That form 404s; the live form is
`.../pgsql-general/<YYYY>-<MM>/` (hyphen, not slash), and the most useful
day-range form is `.../<list>/since/<YYYYMMDDHHMM>/`. The `since/` form is the
one that actually returns a full multi-day thread list; the bare month page
renders truncated to the first day or two via WebFetch. Worth updating the
recipe's URL examples so future runs skip the three 404 probes.
