  The `git.postgresql.org` gitweb substitute works: `a=atom` for the commit

  second consecutive routine (after upstream-watcher #584) to fall back;
  the recipe's `api.github.com` URL forms in "Inputs" are effectively dead in
  the cloud sandbox and should be re-pointed at gitweb.
- **Anomalous night: 3 tree-wide mechanical commits, 109 impacted docs.** The
  recipe's steady-state assumption ("~5-15 impacted docs/day") does not hold
  when upstream lands a whole-tree sweep. The 0-net-line-change filter applied
  here is the honest way to keep such nights from dumping ~100 false-positive
  audit rows onto the single-consumer queue. Suggest the recipe codify this:
  "for a touched path, queue only if net-line-change ≠ 0 OR the doc textually
  references a renamed/removed symbol from the commit."
